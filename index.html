Need an elf for your Christmas party?
Great for any party!

Well – it was the best fluke of chance and luck that happened when blindly hiring a balloon artist for my 40th birthday. Being that it was mostly adults (with some kids peppered in), I was looking for a bit more for the adults in the way of fun entertainment. I found that and MUCH MORE in Sizzle. Not only did she start twisting incredible animals for the kids before she could remove her coat, but I was in awe at the sheer velocity of her craft and connection with an audience. Needless to say, creativity was twisted at every turn from Adult sized wearable Harley motorcycles, sharks with individual teeth, adult style balloons, Angel wings for the kiddos and so much more… I had amazing food, a caricature artist, great drinks, etc. at my party and each and every person who attended continue to note one thing - Sizzle. An impression that left me hiring her again for another party one month later. She is now permanently at the top of my party planning rolodex. Best. Entertainment. Ever.
